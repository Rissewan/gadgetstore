Return & Refund Policy

Our Return Policy

At JJM Gadget Shop, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your product, you may return it within 30 days of purchase for a refund or exchange.

Note: Some products have different return policies:

  • Opened software and digital downloads: Non-refundable
  • Special orders and customized items: Non-returnable
  • Clearance items: Final sale, no returns

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the original packaging with all accessories
  • Unused and in the same condition as received
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
  • Free from any damage, scratches, or signs of wear

Items that don't meet these conditions may be subject to a restocking fee or may not be eligible for return.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our support team within 30 days of purchase at support@jjmgadgetshop.com or call +63 912 345 6789 to initiate your return.
  2. Package your item securely in the original packaging with all accessories.
  3. Include the return form (provided by our support team) inside the package.
  4. Ship the package to our return center at the address provided by our support team.

Note: You will be responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Restocking fees may apply for certain items (typically 15%)

If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days, please contact us.

Exchanges

We gladly exchange items for the same product in a different color or size, or for a product of equal value.

To request an exchange:

  1. Follow the same return process above
  2. Include a note specifying the item you'd like to exchange for
  3. If the exchange item costs more, you'll need to pay the difference
  4. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us immediately at support@jjmgadgetshop.com or call +63 912 345 6789.

We will arrange for a replacement or refund and cover all shipping costs for defective/damaged items.
